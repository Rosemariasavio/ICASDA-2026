Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University)

Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University) has been a beacon of learning and innovation since its inception in 1999 as an affiliated institution under Bangalore University. The Institution is managed by the Christian Missionaries belonging to the order of the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI), St. Joseph Province, Kottayam, Kerala. Marking a significant milestone in its journey, Kristu Jayanti attained the ‘autonomous’ status in the year 2013. As per the notification dated July 08, 2025, vide letter No. 9-13/2025 - U.3(A), Kristu Jayanti has been conferred the status of Deemed to be University under section 3 of UGC Act, 1956, by the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India under General Category.

Kristu Jayanti offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Arts, Science, Management and Commerce, and integrated Law programmes. The institution was reaccredited with the highest grade 'A++' by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) in the Third Cycle of Accreditation in 2021. In the National Institutions Ranking Framework 2025, the institution has been ranked 34th and is one of the top 100 colleges in the country. Moreover, the programmes of the School of Management are internationally accredited by the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs [ACBSP], USA. The Institution was accorded 'DBT Star College' status by the Department of Biotechnology, Ministry of Science & Technology, Government of India in 2020. There are 40 UG and 23 PG programmes.

Department of Statistics, Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University)

Established in 1999, the Department of Statistics at Kristu Jayanti has been dedicated to imparting knowledge in both the theory and application of statistical techniques to undergraduate and postgraduate students across diverse disciplines. The Department actively organizes conferences, seminars, and workshops. Having successfully hosted three National Conferences in the past, the Department is now proud to present its first International Conference.

School of Mathematics and Statistics, Mahatma Gandhi University

Established in 2020 at Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala, the School of Mathematics and Statistics is a vibrant academic unit committed to the advancement of mathematical sciences, statistical methodologies, and their diverse applications. It nurtures analytical thinking and problem-solving skills, equipping students for a wide spectrum of professional careers and higher academic pursuits. The School actively organizes seminars, workshops, and conferences, creating a platform for students and faculty to interact with eminent scholars and practitioners.

Department of Statistics, Bangalore University

Established in 1968 at Bangalore University, Bengaluru, the Department of Statistics commenced with the B.Sc. (Hons.) programme and expanded in 1969 with the M.Sc. programme. The Department integrates theoretical and applied dimensions of Statistics, offering M.Sc. and Ph.D. programmes. It actively collaborates with statistical organizations, R&D establishments, industries, and other academic institutions, advancing statistical knowledge and applications across diverse fields.

Preamble

The International Conference on Applied Statistics, Data Analytics, and Multidisciplinary Research (ICASDAMR–2026), to be held on 12th and 13th February 2026, serves as a platform for academicians, researchers, professionals, and students to share insights and advancements in statistical science and data analytics. Organized by the Department of Statistics, Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University), the conference aims to foster interdisciplinary collaboration, promote innovative research, and highlight the vital role of data-driven approaches in addressing global challenges.
